## [0.1.0] - 2026-07-16

### Added

- Initial Vulkanite colorscheme with editor, syntax, Tree-sitter, LSP semantic-token, diagnostic, diff, and terminal highlights.
- Configurable styles, transparent backgrounds, terminal colors, and public color and highlight callbacks.
- Automatic and explicit highlight integrations for the plugins documented in the README.
- Bundled lualine theme, named 16-color palette, and complete Base16 compatibility mapping.
- Neovim help under `:help vulkanite`.
